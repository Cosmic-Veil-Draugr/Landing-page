About Cosmic Veil Studios

Cosmic Veil is a small group of five student developers. Created to develop Draugr, a capstone project for college. We chose the name because our professor didn't think that Team Draugr was a good name. Our feelings were destroyed but after an arduous journey, we came to this name.
