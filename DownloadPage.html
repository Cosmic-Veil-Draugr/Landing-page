How to Install

Draugr is still very early in development so, players might experience some bugs throughout their play. Keeping that in mind we appreciate you showing an interest in Draugr. For Download instruction and the download button please scroll down.

Once you have downloaded the file, unzip it and click on the DraugrLauncher.exe to open the launcher. In some cases, Antivirus will pop up so we suggest turning off the Anti-Virus to move forward with a smooth install. We are planning on deploying the game to Steam so that we can get rid of all these inconsistencies.


As soon as the launcher is opened it will automatically start downloading the game. Once the download is complete click on the start game button.


Firewall Defender will pop up and to play by yourself make sure the Private network checkbox is ticked and if you want to play with a friend you can also tick the public network.


A console window will pop up with a remote server. Do not close it as it is required to play the game.


Once the game has opened click on Local Connect to enter the game by yourself. You can also connect to a friend's world by entering his public IPv6 or his local IPv4 if you are on the same internet connection by clicking on the custom port button. If you want to play again you have the restart the game and the server.

Controls
WASD - Movement
LMB - Attack
RMB - Block
Space - Kick
E - Heal (Near Shrine)
